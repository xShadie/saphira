[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]STRUCTURE OF THE SYSTEM
[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/systems/common/gaya.png; Y=10; X=446]

[X_PADDING=12]1. Gaya are a currency that allows any player to purchase items in the Gaya Shop.
[X_PADDING=12]2. You can get Gaya from the Merchants of Gaya npc.
[X_PADDING=12]3. To get 1 Gaya you need 10 Gems plus a +0 to +3 Spirit Stone.
[X_PADDING=12]4. Gem refinement is always 100%.
[X_PADDING=12]5. To open the Gaya Shop, go to the Npc Gaya Shop.
[X_PADDING=12]6. The Gaya Shop has 9 slots available of which 6 will be locked, you can unlock the slots
[X_PADDING=12]by purchasing the item Ampliam. Gaya shop from the Npc Mecante di Gaya.
[X_PADDING=12]7. The Gaya Shop updates automatically every 5 hours, you can update the Gaya Shop manually
[X_PADDING=12]by purchasing the Gaya Reset Shop item from the Gaya Mecante npc.


[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]WHERE TO FIND THE GEMS

[X_PADDING=12]1. You can find Gems in the dungeons or bosses of the various maps.
[X_PADDING=12]2. You can find a large amount of Gems in Queen Meley's Chest.
